# Executive Deck Review

Use this skill to critique executive decks.

Read `../../references/executive-briefing-design.md` for the rubric.

## Findings First

Prioritize:

1. Missing or unclear decision/ask.
2. Weak answer-first storyline.
3. Unsupported or unsourced claims.
4. Chart noise or metric confusion.
5. Slides that take too long to parse.
6. Inconsistent design system.
7. Accessibility or brand credibility issues.

## Output

For each finding include severity, slide/section, issue, impact, and fix. End with a boardroom readiness decision: ready, ready with edits, or not ready.
