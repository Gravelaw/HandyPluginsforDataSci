# Executive Deck Review

Use this skill to critique executive decks. Findings come first.

Read `../../references/executive-briefing-design.md` for the rubric.

## Findings Priority

1. Missing or unclear decision/ask.
2. Weak answer-first storyline.
3. Unsupported, unsourced, or inflated claims.
4. Missing domain theme or weak credibility signals.
5. Chart noise, metric confusion, or unlabeled units.
6. Slides that take too long to parse.
7. Generic icon grids, stock-looking visuals, or irrelevant imagery.
8. Accessibility or brand credibility issues.

## Output

For each finding include severity, slide/section, issue, impact, and fix. End with a boardroom readiness decision: ready, ready with edits, or not ready.
