# Training Activity Slides

Use this skill when a training deck needs active learning, practice, feedback, or assessment slides.

Read `../../references/training-deck-design.md` when planning a full module.

## Activity Workflow

1. State the learning objective and concept being practiced.
2. Choose activity type: recall, classify, debug, compare, role-play, scenario, lab, reflection, teach-back, or design critique.
3. Give clear instructions, timebox, materials, constraints, and success criteria.
4. Include answer reveal, worked solution, feedback rubric, or debrief prompt.
5. Use consistent visual mode markers: learn, demo, practice, check, discuss, reflect.
6. Use screenshots, diagrams, code/data snippets, cards, timelines, or realistic domain artifacts when they help the learner act.
7. Keep activity slides visually simple and instructionally complete.

## Gate

A learner should know exactly what to do, how long to do it, what good looks like, and how they will get feedback.
